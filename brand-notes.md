# Angelic Bail Bonds Brand Notes

## Tone
- Warm
- Professional
- Fast-response
- Calm under pressure
- Judgment-free
- Discreet
- Trustworthy

## What the product should feel like
- A real operational tool
- Easy to use under stress
- High-trust
- Polished
- Compassionate without sounding soft or vague
- Clear and direct

## What it should NOT feel like
- Tactical
- Militarized
- Harsh
- Juvenile
- Startup-y
- Generic SaaS
- Tech bro
- Fake legal drama

## Defendant-facing experience
Should feel reassuring, easy, clear, and action-oriented.
The user should instantly know:
- next court date
- next payment due
- how to check in
- how to contact the company

## Admin-facing experience
Should feel operational, organized, and efficient.
It should help staff quickly see:
- who is active
- who missed a check-in
- who has court coming up
- who owes money
- what reminders and notes exist

## Long-term architecture
This app should be built so Angelic Bail Bonds is the default brand, but future bail agencies can rebrand it by changing centralized config values rather than rewriting the app.
