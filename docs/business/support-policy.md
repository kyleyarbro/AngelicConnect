# Support Policy

## Scope of Standard Monthly Support

### Included
- Hosting/runtime health monitoring
- Normal bug triage and bug fixes in existing features
- Minor content/config updates (copy, contact values, theme token adjustments)
- Routine dependency/security maintenance
- Basic deployment assistance for scheduled releases

### Not Included (Custom/Billable)
- Net-new feature builds
- Large workflow redesigns
- New third-party integrations
- One-off data migration/cleanup projects
- Major UI redesign requests

## Response Expectations

### Normal Support
- Acknowledgement target: same business day
- Initial triage target: within 1 business day
- Resolution target: based on severity and scope

### Emergency Support
- Intended for production outage, sign-in failure across users, broken check-in submission, or severe data-access failure.
- Acknowledgement target: as fast as practical under emergency channel agreement.
- Emergency work may incur after-hours or priority fees if outside contracted plan.

## Severity Model
- Critical: core workflow unavailable (login/check-in/admin unusable).
- High: major function degraded with workaround.
- Medium: partial issue, moderate operational impact.
- Low: cosmetic/minor issue.

## Client Responsibilities
- Provide one operational point of contact.
- Report reproducible details (user role, page, time, screenshots).
- Approve custom-scope quotes quickly when issue is outside support scope.

## Service Boundaries
- Support covers the managed platform and configured deployment.
- External outages (carrier issues, third-party provider incidents, DNS host downtime) are managed best-effort and may not be directly controllable.
